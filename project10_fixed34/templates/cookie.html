Cookies Policy

At Farm Fresh, we use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. This page explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your options for managing them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve the website experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To analyse website traffic and usage patterns.
  • To improve site functionality and user experience.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website to function properly.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data to help us improve our site.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and personalise your experience.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

If you have any questions about our cookies, please contact us at info@farmfresh.co.uk.
