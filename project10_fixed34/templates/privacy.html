Privacy Policy

At Farm Fresh, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information such as name, email, and contact details when you register or make a purchase.
  • Usage data like IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders and provide customer service.
  • To send updates and promotional material (only if you opted in).
  • To improve our website and services.

3. Sharing Your Information

We will never sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties for the purpose of fulfilling orders or legal requirements.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at info@farmfresh.co.uk for any privacy concerns.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

Thank you for trusting Farm Fresh with your information.
