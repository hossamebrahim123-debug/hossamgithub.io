Our Story

About The Grand

Est. 1920

A Century of Excellence

The Grand was founded in 1920 by Sir Edward Harrington, a visionary hotelier who believed that true luxury was not simply about opulence, but about creating an atmosphere where every guest felt genuinely at home. From its very first day, The Grand set a standard that others aspired to reach.

Over a century later, that founding philosophy remains at the heart of everything we do. We have welcomed heads of state, celebrated artists, business leaders and travellers from every corner of the world — each one leaving with memories they carry for a lifetime.

Today, The Grand continues to evolve while honouring its heritage. Our team of over 200 dedicated professionals work tirelessly to ensure that every stay exceeds expectation.
